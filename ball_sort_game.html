Privacy Policy

Ball Sort Puzzle

Ball Sort Puzzle ("the Game", "we", "us") is designed to be enjoyed by players of all ages. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Game handles and how. By playing, you agree to this policy.

Effective date: May 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask you to create an account or provide personal details such as your name, email, or phone number to play.

  • Game data stored on your device: your level progress, stars, unlocked items, hints/undos, and your settings (sound, music, haptics). This stays on your device.
  • Information collected automatically by our service providers: to show ads and process purchases, third parties (see Section 4) may process device and advertising identifiers and limited technical data (e.g., device type, app version, general region). We do not control and do not receive your payment card details.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information solely to:

  • run the Game and save your progress and preferences;
  • display advertisements that keep the Game free;
  • process optional in-app purchases (e.g., Remove Ads);
  • fix bugs and improve gameplay.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. In-App Purchases

Optional purchases (such as Remove Ads, hint and undo packs) are handled entirely by the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Those stores process your payment under their own privacy policies — we never see or store your payment information.

4. Advertising

The free version of the Game shows ads provided by Google AdMob. Depending on your region and settings, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

For children and family audiences, we configure ads to be child-appropriate and to use non-personalized (contextual) ads where required by law and store policies.

Learn how Google uses data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

5. Children's Privacy

We care about protecting children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children (under 13, or the minimum age in your country such as 16 in parts of the EEA) beyond what is necessary to run the Game and serve child-appropriate, non-personalized ads.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

6. Your Choices & Controls

  • Local data: uninstalling the Game removes the progress and settings stored on your device. You can also reset progress from within the Game.
  • Ads: you can limit ad personalization in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking).
  • Remove Ads: a one-time in-app purchase disables ads entirely.

7. Data Sharing

We share limited data only with the service providers needed to run the Game: Google AdMob (advertising) and the Google Play / Apple App Store (purchases). Each operates under its own privacy policy. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

8. Data Security & Retention

Your gameplay progress and settings are stored locally on your device. We retain only what is needed to provide the Game. While no method of electronic storage is completely secure, we take reasonable measures to protect information handled by the Game.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (for example under GDPR or the CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information, and to object to certain processing. Because most data stays on your device, you can exercise many of these rights by adjusting device settings or uninstalling. For anything else, contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective date" above and within the app. Continued play after changes means you accept the updated policy.
