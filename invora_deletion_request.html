Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: [EFFECTIVE DATE]

This page explains how to delete your Invora account and the data associated with it, operated by [COMPANY / DEVELOPER LEGAL NAME]. You can delete your account directly in the app, or request deletion by email if you can no longer access the app.

Note: Invora is offline‑first. If you never created an account, your data is stored only on your device — simply uninstalling the app (or clearing its storage) removes it, and there is no cloud account to delete.

Option 1 — Delete your account in the app (fastest)

  1. Open Invora and go to Settings.
  2. Open the Account section.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This permanently deletes your account and its associated cloud data, and signs you out immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot sign in, email us from the email address associated with your account and we will delete it for you:

  • Send an email to [CONTACT EMAIL]
  • Use the subject line “Invora account deletion request”
  • Send it from the email address you used to sign in so we can verify the request.

We process verified deletion requests within 30 days and will confirm by email once complete.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove the personal data linked to your account from our systems, including:

DataWhat happens
Account & authentication (your email / user record) Deleted
Cloud‑synced business data (invoices, estimates, clients, items, expenses) Deleted from the cloud
Shared invoice/estimate links and their view (read‑receipt) records Deleted
Subscription / Pro entitlement records held by us Deleted
Data stored only on your device (offline data) Removed when you delete your account, or when you uninstall the app / clear its storage

What may be retained

We may retain a limited amount of information where we are legally required to, or to complete a legitimate business purpose, for example:

  • Purchase / transaction records: in‑app purchases are handled by Google Play or the Apple App Store; those records are held by the app stores under their policies and are not controlled by us. Manage or cancel subscriptions in your Google Play or Apple account settings.
  • Records required for legal, tax, accounting, or fraud‑prevention obligations, retained only for as long as required and then deleted.
